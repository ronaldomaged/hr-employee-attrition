---

## Project Idea
This project focuses on analyzing HR employee data to understand the factors that contribute to employee attrition.  
The goal is to explore patterns in employee behavior, job satisfaction, salary, overtime, and work environment to identify why employees leave the company and what factors influence employee retention.

## Project Plan

### 1. Research & Analysis
- Understanding the HR dataset
- Identifying key variables related to employee attrition
- Exploring relationships between employee satisfaction, salary, and work conditions

### 2. Data Preparation
- Data cleaning
- Handling missing values
- Removing unnecessary columns
- Preparing the dataset for analysis

### 3. Data Analysis
- Analyzing attrition distribution
- Exploring attrition by department, job role, and salary
- Identifying key drivers behind employee turnover

### 4. Data Visualization
- Creating visualizations to highlight patterns and insights
- Charts such as attrition by department, overtime impact, and salary distribution

### 5. Review & Finalization
- Reviewing the analysis results
- Validating insights
- Preparing the final report

### 6. Final Presentation
- Presenting insights and findings
- Explaining the key factors influencing employee attrition
